UI/UX Design

I start with project discussions to gather requirements and details from the business, conduct user research, and perform competitive analysis. Following this, I create information architecture and user flows, produce low-fidelity wireframes and high-fidelity mockups, and conduct usability testing to evaluate the flow and functionality. Through this iterative process, I identify the optimal design concept.

This process provides an attractive UI/UX mockup with clickable prototypes and a design system for easy scaling and quick development. Additional stages, such as surveys and further UX research may be necessary and will be included as needed. Ultimately, my goal is to deliver results that propel the business forward.
